WHAT IS THIS PAGE
Multi Cluster Equity Pulse ek unified portfolio view hai jo ek saath saare trading clusters aur accounts ka combined equity performance ek single chart mein dikhata hai.

Chahe ek cluster select karo, multiple karo, ya specific accounts filter karo — sab ka combined equity curve ek jagah milta hai.
HOW IT WORKS
01 Cluster Tick ON — Pura cluster aur uske saare accounts chart mein include ho jaate hain
02 Account Tick — Sirf selected accounts ka equity dikhata hai — baaki exclude
03 Range Buttons — Weekly · Monthly · Quarterly · Half-Yearly · Annual time filter
04 Combined Curve — Saare selected trades chronologically merge hoke ek equity curve banate hain. INR trades auto USD mein convert hote hain
CHART COLOR LOGIC
Green Glow — Full Win (2+ scales booked)
Blue Glow — Partial Win (1 scale booked)
Red Glow — Stop Loss hit
Gold — Starting balance point
WHY IT EXISTS
Ek trader ke paas multiple accounts hote hain — demo, live, prop firm. Har ek ka performance alag alag dekhna incomplete picture deta hai.

Yeh page overall portfolio health ek nazar mein dikhata hai — jaise ek fund manager apna entire book ek chart mein dekhta hai.
